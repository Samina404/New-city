About New City Point

Celebrating Pakistani Heritage & Craftsmanship

Our Story

New City Point was born from a deep passion for preserving and celebrating the rich heritage of Pakistani fashion. For generations, our artisans have perfected the art of traditional embroidery, creating pieces that blend timeless elegance with contemporary design.

Each dress in our collection tells a story of dedication, where master craftsmen pour their heart and soul into every stitch, every embellishment, and every intricate detail. From the vibrant bridal lehengas to the graceful formal anarkalis, our creations embody the spirit of Pakistani culture.

We believe that fashion is more than just clothing — it's a celebration of identity, tradition, and artistry. Every piece we create is a testament to the skilled hands that crafted it and the rich cultural tapestry of Pakistan.

The Art of Hand Embroidery

Our signature hand embroidery techniques — including zardozi, gota patti, dabka work, and thread embroidery — are what make each piece truly special. These intricate art forms require years of training and incredible patience.

Each bridal ensemble can take weeks or even months to complete, with artisans carefully placing every sequin, bead, and thread to create breathtaking patterns that shimmer and shine. This dedication to detail is what transforms fabric into wearable art.
